Mary Constable ( c1804 - 18?? )

John is not with the rest of the family on the 1841 census and on the 1851 and beyond Mary is flagged as a widow. I can find no entry in the IGI for John's death. I've seen a burial date of 01 JUL 1833 in other people's trees but no source for it.
