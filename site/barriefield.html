Here are three pictures taken of the students and teachers at the school in Barriefield, S.S. No.1 Pittsburg Township (Barriefield is named after Sir. Robert Barrie), Ontario (just east across the Cataraqui River from Kingston). The common thread is that in each class are some of Thomas and Alice Newbury's children. These are from around 1936-1940.
