    Could this be our Harry?:

    1910.04.21 emigrated to Canada Travelled from Liverpool, England on the Allen Line's S.S. Corsican bound for Montreal Quebec and Quebec, Quebec. From Library and Archives Canada, Reference: RG 76, Microfilm: T-4765: ship's passenger list - they are on page 27. On their ticket: Walter Bester (34) a farmer, Alice Bester (35) and Walter Bester (11). Their destination is Danville, Quebec, they had $200.00 in hand. They are stamped G.T.R. and British Bonus Allowed. Also on board was a Hy Bester (21) butcher/farmer - could this be brother Harry? His destination is stated as Winchmore Hill, which makes no sense and is more likely where he was born. He had $25.00 in hand and was stamped British Bonus Allowed. Number of Souls: 1499 Adults, 274 Children. Steerage Inspection began at 8:00 am, finished at 11:30 am. G.T.R passengers left by special train at 3:00 pm. One family of three was put off at Grosse Isle.
